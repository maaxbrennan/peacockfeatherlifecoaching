Niche Coaching

Niche coaching is about finding your direction and rediscovering our authenticity and the bigger picture (soul plan). We do this by looking at the contrast in our lives, our limiting beliefs and how we break through them. To implement skill sets, how to implement these in the chosen area, what our unique qualities are and how we package this.

Empowerment Coaching

Empowerment coaching is where we identify what keeps us from stepping into our power. We look at limiting beliefs and the stories we keep replaying, and replace these with self-love.

Spiritual Coaching

Spiritual coaching is about getting into vibration with our soul path so we can transcend and connect to our higher self. We use meditation, healing, oracle cards & Access Bars ® to shift limits & learn tools to stay protected, be it psychically, from energy vampires, or toxic relationships. We also look at empathy & what we can attract, the good and the bad.

Relationship Coaching

Relationship coaching, be it work based, family, or intimate relationships is where we look at how we interact with others. We analyse triggers and patterns of behaviour that do not serve us, as well as forgiveness & building new links when things become monotonous and need an injection of life.

For those involved with partners or children with additional needs, we will also look at the impact of stress & when we are dealing with life from a highly stressed place how this affects our ability to respond, instead of react. Guilt can be a huge issue, as even our best sometimes doesn’t seem to hit the spot. This can feel soul destroying, and the need for respite and techniques like EFT & energy healing to use at home to keep us topped up.

Singles Coaching

Singles coaching is for the divorced, widowed or people just looking for a meaningful relationship but keep getting disappointed. We will look at loss, trust, guilt, forgiveness, letting go, confidence, limiting beliefs, hope & how we sometimes use outdated patterns of behaviour that may not serve us.

Woman's Membership Group

The women’s membership group is a safe place to heal your inner world, stop playing small, release limiting beliefs and blame stories so you can transform your outer life within a supportive community that cares.

Transcendence

The upcoming course Transcendence is for spiritual & holistic providers to step into their power as wayshowers. It tackles money mind-set, empathy, unique niches within the competitive industry, authenticity, imposter syndrome, & the reason you need to share your light and how to do so.
