About Peacock Feather Life Coaching

At Peacock Feather Life Coaching, we provide holistic empowerment life coaching, bespoke to our clients needs. We’re based on the South Coast of the UK, in Hampshire.

Peacock Feather Life Coaching is a branch of the Revive holistic tree, which was founded by Kate Rawding in 2016.

About Kate Rawding

I started out providing Reiki and Indian head massage, but spent just as long talking through challenges and together finding solutions to blocks & limitations. I was drawn to enabling others to empower themselves & felt like I’d found my perfect role, so I continued learning & trained in different areas like self-sabotage, psychology, mastery vision quest, Hoʻoponopono, life coaching & so much more.

I’ve swam in the huge sea of limiting beliefs, stayed there quite a while, whilst I clung to my trauma stories that kept me trapped. I get it! But I let go of all my stories and limitations that kept me small and stepped into my power, which in turn led me to empower others.

Another passion of mine is empowering parents of children or adults with additional needs, to create balance & calm amidst the chaos. I have two children with additional needs, a beautiful cocktail of ‘labels’ but both are far more than this, thinkers outside the box, empathic, not afraid to stand in their power.
